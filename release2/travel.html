Why Do I Love Travel?

I am interested in international travel because I have always desired to travel beyond the scope of my surroundings to expand my experiences while ultimately striving to achieve a broad, well rounded and unbiased view of the world. Furthermore, traveling has assisted my growth as an empathetic and entrepreneurial individual because I have had experiences while abroad that have enabled me to think differently and rerame my mindset. Moreover, as young adults who are still in the process of developing our most conclusive perception of ourselves traveling can guide the direction we want to take our lives as we ease into the next stages of it. Similarly, international travel helps activate a sort of self reflection it and acts as an unconventional education. Seeing the world with your own two eyes is such a raw and unmanufactured type of education that is entirely impossible to receive in solely in school.
