Mission Vision 

Mission

Complexion Apparel is an apparel brand designed to empower women to love themselves and their bodies through the clothes that they wear and uplift each other within an inclusive and connected community full of other unique and powerful women.Complexion Apparel clothing not only accentuates the natural shape of every woman but compliments her unique and individualistic style. Our garments will feature sustainably sourced fabric that is structured enough to offer support in all areas of a woman's body but is breathable and soft. Furthermore, at Complexion Apparel, our garments are designed to highlight any body type or shape and are handcrafted to order for the perfect fit.

Complexion Apparel is more than just clothes. Complexion Apparel is a community. Complexion Apparel is your complexion, your body, your beauty.

Vision

In America, beauty equals status, and being dark-skinned has always been a disadvantage. This phenomenon, also known as Colorism, is apparent throughout our society as only a few years ago, embracing your true skin tone was a foreign concept. Yet, as we are moving towards a societal focus of women empowerment, inclusivity, and body positivity Complexion Apparel aims to encourage women to love themselves and their bodies through the clothes that they wear.

Therefore, my vision is that Complexion Apparel offers a unique solution to the problem of body image that plages women globally because it combines an extremely positive message with a tangible item that offers women the ability to look good and feel good in clothes that are made for them.
