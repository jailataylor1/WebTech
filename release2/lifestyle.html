Healthy Lifestyle

For me personally, When I'm not at my healthiest, I can always tell. I feel tired, at times I have stomach aches, and I seem to catch colds. Mentally, I can’t concentrate and at times I feel anxious or depressed. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle usually helps me feel better. It’s pretty easy to make a couple of small changes that can steer continue to keep me in the direction of improved well-being. And once I started implrementing these changes, the success can motivated me to continue to make more positive shifts.

What exactly a “healthy lifestyle”?

When you ask 50 people to define what a “healthy lifestyle” is, and usually you'll recieve 50 different answers. That’s because there’s no one way to be healthy. A healthy lifestyle simply means doing things that make you happy and feel good. For one person, that may mean walking a mile five times a week, eating fast food once a week, and spending virtual or in-person time with loved ones every other day. For someone else, a healthy lifestyle may be training and running two marathons a year, following a keto diet, and never having a sip of alcohol. You get to decide what your healthy lifestyle looks like.
