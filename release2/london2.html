What to Expect when Visiting this City!

  1. Language
    • English is the most spoken language in London and is the national language of the entire United Kingdom!
  2. Currency
    • The primary currency used in London is the Brisith Pound or Sterling.
    • Usually the US Dollar to British Sterling exchange rate is 1 pound per 1.36 US Dollars
  3. Credit Cards and Banks
    • Since London is one of the most global and financial adept cities on Earth it is incredbly easy to have access to your money! ATMs are a commonplace on almost every street and shopping center!
    • Almost all retailers in London accept Matercard, Visa, and/or take Apple Pay!
  4. Climate
    • London's weather is generally mild and at times can become a bit chilly due to the frequent rain!
    • It is almost always worth carrying an umbrella and/or raincoat when out an about in the city to stay dry!
    • London's Cold Season is from December to Feburary, with temperatures usually a few degrees above freezing
    • London's Warm Season is from June to August, with comfortable temperatures near the mid 70s!
